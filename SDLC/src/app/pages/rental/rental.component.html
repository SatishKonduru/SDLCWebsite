Property Listing Management

Our app allows property managers to easily list and manage rental properties. With detailed property descriptions, high-quality images, and virtual tours, prospective tenants can explore properties conveniently from their devices.

Tenant Screening and Applications

Simplify the tenant screening process with our integrated application system. Prospective tenants can submit applications online, and property managers can review and screen applicants efficiently using built-in tools for background checks and credit scoring.

Online Payments and Invoicing

Our rental app supports secure online payments, allowing tenants to pay rent and other fees conveniently through various payment methods. Automated invoicing and reminders ensure timely payments and reduce administrative burdens.

Maintenance Requests

Tenants can easily submit maintenance requests through the app, and property managers can track and manage these requests efficiently. Real-time updates keep tenants informed about the status of their requests.

Lease Management

Manage lease agreements digitally with our comprehensive lease management feature. Property managers can create, sign, and store lease agreements within the app, making the process paperless and more efficient.
